My Personality Test Results

The first picture shows my meyers brigg perosonality test result, the second picture on the right is my learning style test result and the third picture on the bottom shows my creativity test result.

What these results mean for me is firstly that I have strong creativity, extroversive, thinking and judging skills. In a group situation, I would be effective as a delegator of tasks and combined with my strong creative thinking I am able to develop solutions to problems that occur as part of a team. Have strong judgement skills is important because to lead a team, it is important to judge each team member’s ideas and opinions combined with extraversion to have strong awareness of other team members. These skills from these tests show that I am a very strong team player and being a very strong team player is a very important skill in IT because there will be situations where I must collaborate within a team as shown in my ideal job position description. For my learning style, I am more of a visual learner and a auditory learner. Being an auditory learner is a good asset in a team-based situation combined with strong listening skills because taking on feedback from others is very important in a team situation. Onto being a visual learner, it allows me to visualise problems from the bigger picture and I prefer to draw out the problem on a piece of paper to conceptualize the problem at large in order to define an effective solution, while taking on other team members feedback. When forming a team, all these skills are important because an effective team member requires creativity, thinking, intuitive and being extroverted in order to form strong professional relationships. For leadership, having strong critical thinking, judging and listening skills are important because as a team leader these are very important attributes when in a position of leadership because leadership depends on the fundamental principles of understanding others.
