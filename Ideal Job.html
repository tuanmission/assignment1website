What is my Ideal Job in IT?

The position that would be ideal for me is working in helpdesk IT support because I enjoy helping people which is why initially I pursued a healthcare career and fixing things as well. I feel rewarded when I’ve helped someone and I get a really good kick when I troubleshoot and fix something. The skills and qualifications required for this position is firstly having an IT or computer science degree, 3-5 years an IT administrator or consultant and familiarisation with various software platforms including serviceNow, maintaining Vmware and office 365. The last skills required is excellent communication and teamworking skills in order to deal with diverse clients. The current skills I have is my excellent teamworking and communication skills developed throughout my previous studies and professional work experience.

My plan for getting the remaining skills for this position is to continue building my teamwork and communication skills throughout my studies through group assignments and professional combined with volunteer work experience. 

The next step in getting the required skills is to complete this degree and while studying for this degree is to get exposure to the various kinds of platforms that I will be using throughout my IT career through both coursework and further reading about the platforms and software systems. Gaining diverse platform  and software system experience is important because the more platforms that I will be exposed to the more experience I have in dealing with these platforms which I could put in my resume and be familiar with them once I start out to professionally in the field. Besides getting experience on the platforms through coursework, I will also gain more experience on diverse platforms by researching the different kinds of platforms and software systems in order to have high familiarity with many different software systems and platforms used in IT.
