Project idea

 

 

For my project, I would like to follow my passions in automation, AI and robotic technology. The project that I want to develop is automation of the Dandenong to Watergardens railway line as part of the Melbourne Metro Project with the integration of AI and robotic control to ensure a reliable passenger railway service that can cater for an ever growing city which Melbourne is forecasted to be especially in the growth corridors near Watergardens, Caroline Springs and the south eastern suburbs (Victorian Planning Authority, 2019). AI and robotic control will be a major part of this project because the aim of this project is to reduce as much human intervention required to run a busy railway line and integrating technology into the railway line such as real time passenger information, train scheduling, maintenance and streamlined operations (Swiss Federal Railways, 2017).

The main features of this product will include:

·         Automated train control. Automated train control is using driverless technology on this corridor between Watergardens and Dandenong. Driverless technology has been in place around the world and it has been successful in many countries including Australia with the Sydney Metro. With Automated train control, the train driver will not need to manually drive the train along the corridor and can assume a supervisory role of the train where the driver can just open and close the doors. Using automated train control will improve reliability because more trains can run due to automated train control allowing more timeslots, which in return will allow more train services to run (Swiss Federal Railways, 2017).

·         Use of Artificial Intelligence to determine efficient scheduling of trains, timetabling and infrastructure management of the railway line. Artificial Intelligence is becoming more and more prevalent in society (Miailhe,2017). By incorporating artificial intelligence into this railway line, it will streamline operations such as maintenance and scheduling because AI can be used to help determine the most efficient way to schedule the train services for each day to ensure the best possible service to the customers and it can feed into the Automated train control system to ensure that trains are signalled so that as much time slots are freed up as possible to ensure many services can run during peak hours. In infrastructure management and maintenance artificial intelligence can be used to manage this because by collating real time data from rolling stock and infrastructure such as signals and systems AI can determine when pieces of infrastructure may need maintenance so operational management can determine when maintenance can occur to reduce downtime for the railway line and reduce costs on maintenance labour.

·         Incorporation of the use of real time information and data. The automated train control system and AI can be linked to the passenger information system along this railway line. Linking these services together will allow more accurate real time information to passengers because the Automated Train Control system can track the location and schedule of each train along the railway line during its journey which allows real time feedback to the passenger information system so passengers can receive information on when their train will arrive for better convenience for passengers in their commute. Also, disruptions can be better managed as well because the Automated Train Control system can know when a disruption occurs, passengers are informed more accurately about the situation and AI can determine the best course of action to manage the disruption along the railway line such as notifying a maintenance crew, isolating the faulty system or arranging a emergency bus replacement plan. Finally the use of real time data and information can be incorporated into security and safety where if a trespasser is found on the railway line, emergency services can be notified early about the situation and all trains are slowed down on the railway line or suspended until the trespasser is found and removed reducing the chance of a fatality occurring.

The tools required for this project to occur successfully are:

Sourcing an efficient Automatic Control System. An efficient and effective automated train control system on the market can be sourced and tailored to the conditions of this railway line (Bombardier Transportation, 2019). Another alternative is to develop an automated control system for Melbourne; however, this will cost more money.

Development of artificial intelligence for the railway. Obviously for this project to occur successfully an artificial intelligence must be developed for all the automation to run. Just line the ATC, it can be sourced from the open market or can be developed in house for the railway line.

Collating existing data and developing databases to manage the real time data and information required. As the existing Dandenong to Watergardens railway line has tons of big data that needs to be mined and integrated successfully into an information system for the AI and the ATC systems to work effectively, an efficient database and data management system must be developed for the AI and ATC to communicate with that incorporates the existing data already on the railway line such as rolling stock, infrastructure, signals, structures and line data such as landmarks, stations and key locations (SAS Institute, 2019).

The skills required for this to occur successfully are:

Automated Train Control system engineer-An engineer will be required to source and develop the automated train control system that is tailored for Melbourne. The engineer with also manage the day to day operations of the automated train control system (Bombardier Transportation, 2019).

Systems and data analyst-A data and systems analyst will develop a database system and manage the incorporation of the existing data on the railway required for the Automation to occur (GTI Media, 2014).

Integration engineer-An integration engineer will develop solutions to integrate existing technology such as rolling stock and infrastructure into the automated system (GTI Media, 2014).

Cybersecurity expert-Because this automated system like any computer system is susceptible to hacking and threats a cyber security expert will be required to ensure the security of this system because if the security of the system is compromised, it can lead to massive disruptions which can bring the city into an internal emergency, not to mention the breach of customers data (GTI Media, 2014).

Outcomes of this project.

The outcomes of this project are that it will lead to a more efficient and reliable railway line that can  adapt to the needs of an ever growing city by running more train services more often when needed in the growth corridors of Melbourne in the north west and the south east. Furthermore, it can lead to more return for the railway due to reduced costs which can lead to increased profits which can be incorporated back into the railway for continual improvement.
